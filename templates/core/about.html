About GlobeTrek

Your smart travel planning companion

Our Mission

At GlobeTrek, we believe that everyone deserves to explore the world and create unforgettable memories. Our mission is to make travel planning simple, smart, and accessible to everyone, regardless of their budget or experience level.

What We Do

GlobeTrek is an intelligent travel planning platform that helps you create personalized itineraries based on your preferences, interests, and budget. Using advanced algorithms and real-time data from Google Places API, we provide you with:

  • Smart Itinerary Generation: AI-powered daily plans tailored to your interests
  • Budget-Conscious Planning: Stay within your budget with cost-effective recommendations
  • Hotel Recommendations: Find the perfect accommodation for your needs
  • Interactive Maps: Visualize your journey with embedded maps
  • Offline Access: Download your itineraries for offline use

Integrations
